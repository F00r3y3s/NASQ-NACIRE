# NASQ NACIRE Epic Brief

## Product
`NASQ NACIRE` is a UAE-focused industry intelligence platform where organizations publish operational challenges, discover reusable solutions, explore sector activity, and use an AI assistant grounded in internal platform knowledge.

## Problem and Context
Organizations across sectors face similar operational, compliance, safety, coordination, and innovation problems, but the knowledge usually remains fragmented inside companies or ecosystems. The platform exists to make those needs and solutions discoverable in a structured way while preserving confidentiality where needed.

## Who This Serves
- Public visitors who want to browse challenges, solutions, analytics, and use AI for discovery.
- Verified company members who want to submit challenges, publish solutions, manage drafts, and use AI to assist authoring.
- Admins and moderators who govern publication, taxonomy quality, trust, and platform integrity.

## V1 Outcome
V1 turns the artifact HTML from a static prototype into a working product with six live surfaces:
- Dashboard
- Browse Challenges
- Submit Challenge
- Solutions
- AI Assistant
- Analytics

## Core Product Rules
- The artifact HTML is the visual source of truth for fonts, palette, shell, and overall product feel.
- Challenges and solutions are separate entities.
- Challenges require review before publication.
- Verified-member solutions publish immediately in v1.
- Anonymous challenges are supported through a platform relay.
- The AI assistant is public-facing and grounded only in internal platform data in v1.
- Signed-in verified members can turn AI conversations into challenge drafts.
- Company profiles are simple public profiles in v1.
- Analytics is public; moderation and governance are protected admin workflows.
- Sector taxonomy must include the artifact sectors plus `Police & Civil Defense`, `Aviation`, and `Solar & Energy`.

## Success Criteria
- The built product matches the artifact visually with minimal interpretation.
- Public users can browse real challenge and solution records and use the AI assistant meaningfully.
- Verified members can submit challenges, publish solutions, and manage drafts cleanly.
- Admins can govern publication and taxonomy quality without public-surface workarounds.
- The codebase stays secure, clean, maintainable, and ready for docs-grounded implementation.
